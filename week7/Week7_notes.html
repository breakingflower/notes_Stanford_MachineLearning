Choosing SVM Paramters

Regularization

  • C=1λC=\frac{1}{\lambda}C=λ1​.
  • Large C (little regularization): lower bias, high variance. (more prone to overfitting)
  • Small C (large regularization): higher bias, low variance. (more prone to underfitting)

Sigma

  • σ2\sigma^2σ2
  • Large σ2\sigma^2σ2: features fif_ifi​ vary more smoothly. Higher bias, lower variance. (gaussian kernel falls off more smoothly (gets wider)).
  • small σ2\sigma^2σ2: features fif_ifi​ vary abruptly. Higher variance, lower bias. (gaussian falls off very rapidly).

If you have a problem that is overfitting on the training data, decreasing CCC and increasing σ2\sigma^2σ2 would be reasonable steps.
